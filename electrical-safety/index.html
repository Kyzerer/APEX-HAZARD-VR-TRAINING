How to Interact

Controls (Desktop Mode)

  • Movement: Use W, A, S, D keys or Arrow keys to walk around the room.
  • Look Around: Hold Left-Click and drag mouse inside the viewport.
  • Interact: Click on interactive items (highlighted in cyan/red border) with the cursor circle.

Controls (VR Headset Mode)

  • Look Around: Move your head.
  • Interact: Align the center gaze cursor over the target objects for a short time to click, or squeeze the controller trigger.

Training Objectives

Complete safety tasks step-by-step across 6 distinct modules to gather experience points (XP). Maintain safe procedures to prevent injuries. Wrong/unsafe operations will deduct XP or force a scenario reload, accompanied by detailed educational descriptions of what went wrong.

About VR Simulator

Academic Safety Simulator

This Virtual Reality Electrical Safety Training Simulator is designed for engineering laboratories, schools, and professional safety induction courses.

By providing immersive interactions, students gain concrete understanding of Lockout Tagout (LOTO) protocols, voltage thresholds, proper insulating tools, extinguishing agent types, and safety actions during cardiac arrests/electric shock emergencies.

Built completely utilizing standard web technologies, allowing execution on standard smartphones, PCs, laptops, and standalone VR gear.
